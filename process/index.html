Let's start a project together

Our work process involves translating research into solutions for you - the client.

01. Ideation

We partner with startups, enterprises, and innovators to develop viable product ideas with clear development and manufacturing goals.

02.Breadboard Prototyping

We use our extensive hardware platform experience to provide affordable prototyping and R&D costs with fair mass production clauses.

03. Enclosure Design

We design a suitable enclosure for your product based on the functional requirements, mounting style and desired aesthetics.

04. Alpha Prototype

Based on the breadboard prototype and enclosure design, we build an Alpha prototype that allows us and the user to test the product in the field. This enables us to get first hand feedback from the target customers/users, which is used to improve on the product design before moving to the next steps.

05. Electronic Schematic Design

We design the electronic circuit for your product. We use cost effective and readily-available parts during the design process. This reduces the production cost of the final product and ensures that the mass production run will not be affected by chip shortages.

06. Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Design

Using the electronic schematics and the enclosure design we are able to develop a PCB which incorporates all the electronic components that are needed to make the product work.

07. Optimization and Design For Manufacture(DFM)

Once we are satisfied with the overall design of the product we optimize it for manufacturing. Some of the tasks involve making enclosures moldable and certification of the product. Our DFM practices reduce your manufacturing cost and time, ensuring you get to market as quickly as possible and at an affordable price.

08. Manufacturing

We maintain strong relationships with PCBA and product assembly factories, as well as key electronic component distributors to ensure a seamless mass production process.
