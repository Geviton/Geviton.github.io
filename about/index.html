About Us

Breathe life into your ideas.

The Mission

To be a leader in hardware innovation across the world for electronic products that bring revolutionary ideas to life.

The Vision

To innovate mass production of flagship products and provide world class product design services for building and scaling electronic products

The Story

Geviton as a company came into existence out of the need to build capacity for local innovation of electrical product design services. We design, prototype, and develop electrical products that solve problems in the modern world. Geviton was founded on a manufacturing contract focusing on industrial automation being an Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) and Original Design Manufacturer (ODM). The company has a rich background in hardware innovation with more than 10 years experience working in the field, generating traction in creating specialised IP for electronic design and manufacturing for clients across the globe.
